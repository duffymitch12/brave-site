Welcome to Helmet

A Project to Instill Bravery in Everybody

My aim for this project was to take a simple word "helmet" and figure out what it really meant. Through thinking I found that helmets signify protection and invulnerability in many ways. I often visualize a soldier running into battle with bravery and pride.

I decided to take that definition/symbolism and do the opposite. I wanted to display people talking about being invulnerable in a vulnerable way. To almost create a open and welcoming community/platform of people sharing and viewing what bravery means and what a brave act might look like for these individuals.
